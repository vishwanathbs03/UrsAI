# PROJECT SUMMARY

Atlas AI is an AI Decision Intelligence Platform designed for MSMEs.

The platform helps MSMEs make better business decisions through:

• Business Digital Twin
• Business MRI
• AI Decision Engine
• Scenario Simulation
• Executive Decision Brief

The platform does not replace ERP systems or marketplaces.

It acts as an intelligent decision layer above existing business systems.
