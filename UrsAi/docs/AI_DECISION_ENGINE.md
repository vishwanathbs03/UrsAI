# AI Decision Engine

Version 1.0

------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

The AI Decision Engine is responsible for transforming business data into actionable intelligence.

It does not merely answer questions.

It evaluates business conditions, identifies gaps, predicts consequences, and recommends actions.

------------------------------------------------------------

# Inputs

Business Information

- Business Type
- Industry
- Product Category
- Country
- Revenue Range
- Employees
- Manufacturing Capacity

Compliance Information

- Certifications
- Licenses
- Registrations

Operational Information

- Packaging
- Sustainability
- Production

Business Goals

- Export Country
- Growth Goal
- Investment Goal

------------------------------------------------------------

# AI Processing Pipeline

Step 1

Validate input completeness.

↓

Step 2

Generate Business Digital Twin.

↓

Step 3

Run Business MRI.

↓

Step 4

Identify weaknesses.

↓

Step 5

Generate Readiness Scores.

↓

Step 6

Generate recommendations.

↓

Step 7

Run simulations.

↓

Step 8

Generate Business Report.

------------------------------------------------------------

# Output

The engine produces:

- Business Health Score
- Export Readiness Score
- Compliance Score
- Trust Score
- Risk Summary
- Opportunity Summary
- Action Plan
- Simulation Results

------------------------------------------------------------

# Design Principle

Every AI response must answer:

"What is the next best action for this business?"

If a recommendation does not help the business move forward, it should not appear in the report.
