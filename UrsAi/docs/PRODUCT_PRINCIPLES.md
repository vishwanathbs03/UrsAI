# PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

## Principle 1

Never ask users for unnecessary information.

---

## Principle 2

Every AI recommendation must include an explanation.

Users should never wonder:

"Why?"

---

## Principle 3

Every report must recommend an action.

Never end with analysis alone.

---

## Principle 4

The platform should reduce uncertainty.

Not simply provide more information.

---

## Principle 5

Every screen should answer one question.

Never overwhelm users.

---

## Principle 6

The AI should behave like an experienced export consultant.

Not a chatbot.

---

## Principle 7

Every interaction should move the MSME one step closer to global readiness.
