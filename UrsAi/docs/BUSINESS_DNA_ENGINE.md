# BUSINESS DNA ENGINE

Version 1.0

--------------------------------------------

## Vision

Every MSME is unique.

Instead of treating all businesses equally, the platform generates a Business DNA that captures the essential characteristics of the enterprise.

The Business DNA becomes the foundation for every AI decision.

--------------------------------------------

## Business DNA Components

Industry

Product Type

Business Size

Production Capacity

Financial Stability

Compliance Maturity

Technology Adoption

Digital Presence

Sustainability

Export Experience

Market Position

Risk Profile

Growth Intent
